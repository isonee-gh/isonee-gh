About us

A practice built on a family's word, kept over time.

“Insurance is not only about purchasing a policy — it’s about having reliable support when it needs to be reviewed, renewed, serviced or claimed upon.”

iSonee is a family-run insurance advisory and servicing practice. Our family's association with the insurance profession spans multiple generations, with agency relationships across several insurance companies. Today, we continue to assist customers with insurance planning, renewals, policy servicing and claims support.

Over the years, our approach has remained simple: understand the customer's requirements, explain the available options, and assist wherever support is needed — before the policy is bought, and long after.
